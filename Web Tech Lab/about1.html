About Us

The International Book Fair is a premier event celebrating literature from around the globe.

Our mission is to promote literacy and the love of reading by building a community of readers.

Our History

The International Book Fair was founded in 2005 by a group of book lovers who wanted to share their passion with the world.

Since then, the fair has grown into an annual event that attracts authors, publishers, and readers from all over the world.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated volunteers who work year-round to make the International Book Fair a success.

We are always looking for new members to join us in our mission to promote literacy and the love of reading.
